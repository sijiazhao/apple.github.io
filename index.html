Apple Gathering Task

Overview

Participants decide whether to accept or reject apple-gathering offers that vary in effort and reward, measuring effort-based decision-making.

Calibration

  • 2 rounds of max-speed tapping (5 s each)
  • Best round sets your personal maximum tap rate

Trial Structure

  • 4 effort levels — 50%, 65%, 80%, 95% of max tap rate
  • 4 reward levels — 1, 5, 10, or 20 apples
  • Full factorial design (16 unique conditions per block)

Each Trial

  • See an effort gauge + reward offer
  • Accept or Reject the offer (no time limit)
  • If accepted: 50% chance of an effort test
  • Effort test: tap above the target rate for 8 s
  • Success earns the apples; failure earns nothing

Data Output

  • Per-trial CSV with decision, RT, tap rate, and outcome
